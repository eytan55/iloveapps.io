How to Create Tasks by Voice

Speak naturally, Voxa understands priorities, due dates, and more

1

Download Voxa Tasks

Get Voxa Tasks free from the App Store. Works on iOS 18.1+ with iPhone and iPad.

Free version: 10 tasks, voice creation, 14 languages, local AI. Pro at €5.99/year for unlimited tasks and iCloud sync.
2

Tap the Microphone

Open Voxa Tasks and tap the floating microphone button. One tap — that's it. No menus, no friction.

3

Speak Your Task

Say what you need to do, naturally. Voxa understands:

Examples:
"Buy groceries high priority tomorrow"
"Call the doctor Monday morning"
"Remind me to water the plants every week"
Voxa extracts: Title, priority, due date, and recurrence automatically.
4

Review and Save

Voxa shows a preview of your task. Review the details, edit if needed, then tap Save.

5

Done! Voxa Remembers for You

Your task is saved instantly. Voxa confirms: "Done! I'll remember for you."

Get reminded at the right time so you can focus on the present. Your mind is free.
