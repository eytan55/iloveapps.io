How to Add Calendar Events by Voice

Speak naturally, Voxa understands dates, times, locations, and more

1

Download Voxa Calendar

Get Voxa Calendar free from the App Store. Works on iOS 18.1+ with iPhone and iPad.

Free version: 10 events, voice creation, 14 languages, local AI. Pro at €5.99/year for unlimited.
2

Tap the Microphone

Open Voxa and tap the floating microphone button. One tap activates voice mode — no menus, no friction.

3

Speak Naturally

Say your event in natural language. Voxa understands:

Examples:
"Lunch with Sarah tomorrow at 12:30 at Vapiano"
"Doctor appointment next Tuesday at 10am"
"Team meeting every Monday at 9"
Voxa extracts: Title, date, time, duration, location, and recurrence automatically.
4

Review and Confirm

Voxa shows a visual preview of your event. Review the details, edit if needed, then tap Save.

Unlike Siri: Voxa lets you see and edit before saving — no blind acceptance!
5

Done!

Your event is added instantly. Voxa confirms with voice feedback: "Done. I've added Lunch with Sarah."

View your events in Day, Week, or Month view. Set reminders to never miss anything important.
